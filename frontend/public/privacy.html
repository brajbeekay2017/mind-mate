1. Introduction

Welcome to Mind Mate ("App," "we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, user ID, and authentication credentials provided during sign-up and login.
  • Health & Wellness Data: Mood entries, stress levels, breathing exercise data, and wellness challenge progress.
  • Google Fit Data: Steps, calories, and other fitness metrics when you authorize Mind Mate to access your Google Fit account.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including features accessed, time spent, and actions performed.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address.

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Process your mood and stress level entries.
  • Display personalized wellness recommendations.
  • Integrate with Google Fit to provide fitness tracking insights.
  • Generate analytics and improve our services.
  • Send you transactional emails and service updates.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or comply with a legal obligation.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Google Fit Integration

Mind Mate integrates with Google Fit to provide you with personalized wellness insights. When you connect your Google Fit account:

  • You authorize Mind Mate to access your fitness data (steps, calories, heart rate, etc.).
  • Your data is used solely to enhance your wellness experience within Mind Mate.
  • You can disconnect Mind Mate from Google Fit at any time through your Google Account settings.
  • We do not share your Google Fit data with third parties without your explicit consent.
